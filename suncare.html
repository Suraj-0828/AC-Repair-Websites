KCPL’s – AVOB (Butyl Methoxy Dibenzoyl Methane – Avobenzone)

CAS No. 70356-09-1

KCPL’S - Avobenzone is an oil-soluble non-toxic chemical that can absorb light across the spectrum. It is a sunscreen that works by absorbing light unlike other sunscreens that emit light. It absorbs light and converts it into energy that is less harmful to the skin, and is one among the rare sunscreens that absorbs both-UVA and UVB rays. Avobenzone is the best alternative for inorganic sunscreens such as zinc oxide and titanium oxide.

White to pale yellow powder, an oil-soluble ingredient used in sunscreen products to absorb the full spectrum of UVA rays. It aids in the prevention of sunburn. It enables broadband protection when combined with effective UVB filters and can also contribute to SPF.

CAS No. 27503-81-7
